// 01. about

Curious by nature.
Offensive by trade.

I'm a security researcher focused on offensive security — finding and validating vulnerabilities in the systems and architectures that organizations rely on most. My work spans web applications, APIs, cloud environments (Azure, AWS, GCP), and Active Directory.

My approach is research-driven and iterative. I spend time understanding how systems are built before looking for where they break. I document findings carefully and collaborate with development teams to make remediation actually land.

Beyond the technical side, I'm interested in how adversaries think — which is why I incorporate red team tactics, threat emulation, and MITRE ATT&CK into assessments. I also manage vulnerability disclosure programs and enjoy the security community through CTFs and continuous learning.

When I'm not testing, I'm writing tooling in Python and PowerShell, studying new attack paths, or working toward the next cert.
